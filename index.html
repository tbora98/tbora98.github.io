About

I am a PhD student in the Photonics Systems Research Group (PSRG) at Michigan State University advised by Prof. Thomas Clark. My research focuses on RF photonics for millimeter‑wave systems, including comb‑based communications and sensing, integrated microwave photonic filters, and photonic circuits for AI‑assisted signal processing.

Research Interests

  • Microwave photonics for mmWave communications and sensing
  • Ring resonators, CROW filters, and envelope detection
  • PIC design and co‑simulation (Lumerical · MATLAB · Python)
  • Electromagnetics, RF/Microwave engineering, and signal processing
